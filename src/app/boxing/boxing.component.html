What is ABS Exercises?

Abdominal exercises are key for developing a strong and defined core, contributing to stability, posture, and overall athleticism. These exercises target various muscles in the abdomen, including the rectus abdominis, obliques, and transverse abdominis. Movements range from crunches and leg raises that focus on the front of the abs to twists and side bends that work the obliques. A comprehensive ab routine incorporates exercises targeting all these areas for balanced core strength.
